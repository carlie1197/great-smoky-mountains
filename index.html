Great Smoky Mountains National Park

University of Kentucky Geography

The United States has 63 protected areas known as national parks that are operated by the National Park Service. National parks are designated for their natural beauty, unique geological features, diverse ecosystems, and recreational opportunities.

The Great Smoky Mountains National Park was established in 1940. This map is intended for use by visitors to the park and displays all hiking trails, as well as trailheads (in yellow) and campsites (in blue).

Tools used in this project
